About COSYWO

A trusted name in precision industrial equipment — manufacturing and exporting excellence since 1960.

Our Story

Trusted Name Since 1960

COSYWO is a well-accepted, leading name in the manufacturing of industrial equipment. Established by Shrikant B. Bhansali — trained abroad at ICI Ltd. Manchester, England and Peder Mortensen a/s. of Denmark — COSYWO was built on a foundation of world-class engineering knowledge and a commitment to serving Indian industry.

Over six decades, we have grown into a trusted supplier for industries ranging from paint and cosmetics to food processing, pharmaceuticals, and textile manufacturing.
